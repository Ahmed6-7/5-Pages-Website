Our Services

The Sindh Police plays a vital role in maintaining law and order across the province of Sindh. Their core services
include crime prevention, investigation, traffic management, and rapid emergency response through helplines like Madadgar-15.
The department also offers specialized units such as Women Protection Cells, Anti-Terrorism Squads, and Crime Scene Investigation
Teams, ensuring modern and effective policing. With a focus on public safety, transparency, and community engagement
Sindh Police continues to serve as a trusted guardian for the people of Sindh.
